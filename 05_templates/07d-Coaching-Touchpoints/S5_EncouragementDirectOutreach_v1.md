# S5 — Encouragement for Direct Outreach

**Subject:** A Simple Step to Boost Employer Responses

---

Hi ${Contacts.First Name},

Good day. I appreciate your feedback. As I understood, you are experiencing some typical challenges that people experience before learning how to get consistent employer feedback.

Job hunting often feels complicated, but it really comes down to two key milestones: employer responses and job interviews. Without a response, there's no interview, so if online applications aren't working, it may be time to start direct conversations with employers.

It's natural to feel discouraged when there's no feedback, but don't forget your value. Your skills are strong, your preparation is done, and you've already completed your Video CV. Now it's about exposure.

A simple 30-second call (just a friendly intro) can spark real interest. Reach out to 10–15 employers over the next two weeks, and you'll gain momentum, feedback, and maybe even an opportunity.

If you need help with scripts, targeting, or anything else, I'm here to support you.
