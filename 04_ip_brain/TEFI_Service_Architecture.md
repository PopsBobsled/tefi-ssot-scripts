## The Core Principle

> **Deal stages are atomic units.**  
> Deal stages combine into product categories (T1–T4).  
> Product categories combine into pipeline proposals.

A CV step in T4 is the same Zoho deal stage as in T1. The stage code is shared — the source material and AI prompts differ because the candidate's evidence base improves at each tier. The pipeline tracks process, not product version.

## T3a — Strategic Profile Programme

*(Previously called "Advanced Strategic")*

**Purpose:** Deep evidence capture and market intelligence for high performers whose value is complex and hard to communicate quickly. The emphasis is on *clarity* — making a strong, credible profile legible to NZ/AU employers fast.

**Who it's for:** Professionals with rich, complex careers who are being overlooked not because of their calibre but because their profile doesn't land clearly. Managers, specialists, senior contributors.

**Not included:** Visual assets (T3b), ongoing job support (T4).

**What is delivered:**
- Premium CV Plus (10-point TEFI methodology, ATS-safe, fast-scan)
- Skills, Achievements and Metrics Library — structured evidence capture from career history
- Career Evidence Inventory — organised proof base for interviews and applications
- Cover Letter System — reusable framework, not a single letter
- Labour Market Prospects Report — NZ/AU sector demand and role fit analysis
- Job Target Segmentation Report — where to aim, ranked by fit and opportunity
- Launch/Activation Plan — practical weekly action guide

**Sessions:** 2–3 live sessions (Extraction + Positioning / Evidence Review / Launch Planning)

**Distinguishing feature:** The market intelligence outputs (Labour Market Prospects Report, Job Target Segmentation Report) are exclusive to T3a and T4. This is the programme for candidates who need to understand the landscape before they start applying, not just improve their documents.

## Stage Code System

Stage codes follow a naming convention that reveals their tier-family and sequence:

| Prefix | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `FP_` | Full Programme stage (shared across tiers) |
| `EE_` | Easy Entry / activation-focused stage |
| `S1` | Session/phase 1 group |
| `S2` | Session/phase 2 group |
| `S3` | Session/phase 3 group |
| `S1a`, `S1b`... | Sub-steps within a phase |

**Key rule:** The same stage code appears in multiple pipelines. Content delivered at that stage differs by tier — but the process step is identical. This is intentional and correct.

## What This Means for Zoho and n8n

- `Client_Activity_Status` in Zoho maps to tier-level status, not deal stage
- Deal stages are tracked inside the Deals module pipeline, not the Contacts module
- The same deal stage name in two pipelines = same process step, same Zoho stage code
- Career Vault tracks the candidate's evidence depth — Zoho tracks the deal process
- The two systems are linked by `Person_ID` and email as match key
