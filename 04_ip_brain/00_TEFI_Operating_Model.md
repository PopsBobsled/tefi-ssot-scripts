## PURPOSE

This document defines the foundational principles, values, and operating framework that drive all TEFI client interactions, service delivery, and output quality across all channels (email, proposals, chatbot, consulting).

---

## THE TEFI PROMISE

TEFI exists to translate skilled migrant profiles into viable, honourable pathways to New Zealand and Australia.

**Core principles:**
- **Honest calibration:** We tell clients what the market will actually do, not what they want to hear
- **Principle-driven communication:** Every interaction reflects the same core values, whether it's an email, a report, or a conversation
- **Continuous improvement:** We learn from every client interaction and embed those learnings into our operating system
- **Source-of-truth discipline:** All claims, metrics, and advice are grounded in actual evidence from CV, client replies, and current market data

## THE IP BRAIN LAYER

The IP Brain is where we capture and evolve **universal principles** — the archetypes that govern how TEFI communicates and delivers value.

### Structure

```
IP Brain
├── 00_TEFI_Operating_Model.md (this file — foundational framework)
├── 01_Communication_Principles.md (archetypes for all outputs)
├── 02_Client_Lifecycle_Model.md (where and when each principle applies)
├── 03_Signal_Registry.xlsx (master log of all discovered signals)
└── 04_Exception_Rules.md (when and why principles bend)
```

### How it works

1. **Instances emerge** in Email-Brain, Tier 1 Pipeline, proposals, etc.
2. **Signals are discovered** — you tag with `claude-📚-learn-my-better-draft`
3. **I extract the signal** and log it to IP Brain
4. **The principle is abstracted** and added to Communication_Principles or Exception_Rules
5. **Email-Brain references IP Brain** — consistency across all outputs
6. **Future outputs use updated principles** — continuous improvement

## TEFI CLIENT PROFILE

**Who we serve:**
- Skilled professionals outside NZ/AU considering migration
- Typically 10+ years experience, mid-to-senior level
- Unaware of how their skills translate to NZ/AU market language

**What they need:**
- Clarity on viability of their profile
- Honest income expectations (arrival, established, 5-year)
- A credible career trajectory they can plan toward
- Confidence that migration is achievable
