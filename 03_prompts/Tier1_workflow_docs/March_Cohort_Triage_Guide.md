# March Cohort Triage Guide
**Last updated:** 2026-04-13
**Cohort:** ~30 leads, March 5–24 2026
**Status:** All past D10. Many past D40. Awaiting classification.

---

## Purpose

Work through each lead row in the Tier 1 Registry and assign one of four statuses. This guide gives you the decision logic for each. Once classified, the nudge system knows what to do next.

---

## The Four Outcomes

| Status | Meaning | Next action |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Dead** | No realistic prospect of conversion | No further contact. Log exit reason. |
| **On Hold** | Timing not right — may return | No contact until they re-engage or DHiringBubble season |
| **DHiringBubble** | Good prospect, just needs the right moment | Queue for Spring (Sep/Oct) or Post-Holiday (Feb/Mar) nudge |
| **Re-engage Now** | Signs of life — worth one more direct touch | Send a tailored email this week |

---

## Decision Logic — Work Through This for Each Lead

### Step 1: What do you know about them?

Ask yourself: do you have enough signal to make a call, or is this a blank?

- **Strong signal** (you remember the conversation, know their situation, know their target role) → proceed to Step 2
- **Weak signal** (you only have their name and a CV, no real conversation happened) → default to **On Hold** unless something in their CV or intake responses suggests strong fit

---

### Step 2: Why didn't they convert?

Think back or check any notes. The most common reasons:

| Reason | Likely status |
|--------|--------------|
| Price was the barrier | DHiringBubble — price sensitivity often drops when urgency is high |
| Timing — not ready to move yet | On Hold or DHiringBubble |
| Already found another solution | Dead |
| Went quiet with no explanation | On Hold (assume timing, not rejection) |
| Expressed clear disinterest | Dead |
| Life event (job loss, family, visa issue) | On Hold — check back at bubble |
| Never replied to W1 at all, no D3/D10 sent | Re-engage Now with D3 or D10 template |

---

### Step 3: How long since last touch?

| Days since last touch | Signal |
|----------------------|--------|
| D10–D30 | Still warm — On Hold or Re-engage Now |
| D30–D60 | Cooling — DHiringBubble or Dead |
| D60+ | Cold — DHiringBubble only if strong original signal, otherwise Dead |
| D40+ and never replied to anything | Dead unless you have strong reason to believe otherwise |

---

### Step 4: What's their immigration situation?

This is the most important filter. Ask: is this person actually going to move?

- **Visa in hand or actively applying** → high intent → DHiringBubble minimum, Re-engage Now if timing is right
- **Exploring options, no visa yet** → medium intent → On Hold or DHiringBubble
- **No visa pathway clear** → low intent → Dead or On Hold (long horizon)
- **Already in NZ/AU** → different product need → flag separately, do not put in standard nudge sequence

---

### Step 5: Make the call

Use this summary table:

| Profile | Status |
|---------|--------|
| Strong fit + visa in hand + went quiet on timing | Re-engage Now |
| Strong fit + visa in progress + timing barrier | DHiringBubble |
| Medium fit + no visa + timing unclear | On Hold |
| Weak signal + D40+ + no replies | Dead |
| Expressed disinterest or found another solution | Dead |
| Good conversation + price barrier | DHiringBubble |
| Never replied to anything, D3/D10 not yet sent | Send D3 now |

---

## What to Record in the Registry

For each lead you triage, update these columns:

| Column | What to enter |
|--------|--------------|
| X — Lead Status | Dead / On Hold / [keep as Lead if re-engaging] |
| Y — Status Reason | One line: why you made this call |
| Z — Last Status Change | Today's date |
| AY — Exit Reason | (Dead only) price / timing / disinterest / no response / found alternative |
| AZ — Exit Date | (Dead only) today's date |
| BA — Reactivation Candidate | Yes/No — if Dead but has strong profile, mark Yes for future DHiringBubble |
| AR — Gmail Label | Apply TEFI/T1-Nudge-Bubble if queued for seasonal nudge |

---

## Special Cases

**"Never replied to W1 — D3 and D10 never sent"**
Check column AB (Nudge 1 Date). If blank, you haven't sent D3 yet. These are not truly cold — send D3 now using the template. Do not classify as Dead until D3 and D10 have both been sent with no response.

**"I remember them but can't recall the details"**
Check their intake questionnaire responses if available, or their CV in their folder. If nothing is there, On Hold is the safe default — you're not writing them off, just parking them.

**"Strong profile but completely silent"**
Mark as DHiringBubble + Reactivation Candidate = Yes. The Spring bubble (Sep/Oct) is your best shot at reactivating a silent high-value lead. The seasonal urgency does work that a cold email won't.

## After Triage

Once all 30 leads are classified:
- Dead leads: no further action needed
- On Hold leads: revisit at next DHiringBubble season
- DHiringBubble leads: add to a separate list (start building it now) — you'll pull from this list in late August for Spring bubble
- Re-engage Now leads: use D3 or D10 template this week

**Target:** Complete triage in one session. 30 leads at ~2 minutes each = approximately 1 hour.
