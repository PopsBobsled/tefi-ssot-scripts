## Usage Notes

- D3 and D10 are sequential — D3 goes first, D10 only if D3 also received no reply
- After D10 with no reply, status moves to On Hold or Dead — do not send further nudges unless DHiringBubble season applies
- DHiringBubble templates are seasonal and manual — Tate identifies candidates from registry, not automated
- Active client versions require one personalised sentence — do not send without it
- Log every send in the registry: Nudge Date, Nudge Type, update Last Touch Date
- Apply Gmail label immediately after sending
